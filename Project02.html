This is my recent assignment which collaborates with "KOI Thé” to create a promotional design project.

Aside from that, the purpose of this assignment is to bring out the message and raise public awareness about the 17 Goals listed in the United Nation (UN)'s Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). Among the UNSDG goals, I picked Goal 14: Life Below Water as my theme and I try my best to convey the message of “Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development”.
